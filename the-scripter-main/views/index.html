What is Scripter ?

Scripter = Scripts + Executor. Scripter is an online web application for execution of your programs in cloud servers for multiple days. Now you don't have to keep your Laptop and CPU running for hours. Just upload your programs and click on "Run". We will execute your scripts in cloud.

We will never read your programs. But for security reasons our Bots will check the presence of few modules in your code.

To offer best security we will never store your passwords in cookies and you will be logged out automatically if the website is closed.
